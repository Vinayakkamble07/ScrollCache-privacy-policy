Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Built for researchers, marketers, and curious people who want to remember what they scroll past.

💾

One-click save

A save button appears directly on every post. Click it and the data goes straight to your Sheet — author, caption, URL, timestamp, all of it.

📊

Your own Google Sheet

All saves land in a spreadsheet in your own Google Drive. No third-party database. You own your data, always.

🔍

Duplicate detection

Already saved that post? ScrollCache checks before saving and skips duplicates automatically.

🚫

Zero tracking

No analytics, no telemetry, no crash reports. We have no server to send data to. It simply doesn't happen.

📸

Ad metadata capture

Even when Facebook hides the permalink, we save the advertiser name and caption so the ad isn't lost.

⚡

Works on the feed

Scroll your feed naturally. The save button appears inline — no copy-pasting URLs, no switching tabs.

Set up in 60 seconds
